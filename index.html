I'm a Computer Science graduate student at New York University. My research goal is to improve the trustworthiness of artificial intelligence systems and ensure that AI systems smarter than humans align with human interests. My research takes shape in three key directions:

  • Scalable oversight for supervising unreliable experts with multiagent debate.
  • Large model evaluation for extreme risks, exploring adversarial attacks and jailbreaking of large models.
  • Machine reasoning for improving commonsense reasoning and problem-solving capabilities.

In addition, I have previously worked on and am interested in:

  • AI for social good, especially in the healthcare and sustainability domains [Remote Sensing] [Science of Total Environment] [more..]
  • Explainable AI for understanding disparity in Post-Hoc explanation[FAccT 2024]
