Data science & AI application in environmental health

Big data and AI are revolutionizing the environmental health sector. The environmental health concern with all aspects of the natural and built environments that may affect human health. Though environmental health is a vast topic, we are primarily concerned with the key component such as heat, climate & its effects on health, air pollution, radiation, solid waste, and toxic material management. The availability of big data, better machine learning, and computation algorithm make it easier to design a better environmental health system.
