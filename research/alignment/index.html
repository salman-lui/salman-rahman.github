Combining Machine Learning and Satellite Imagery

Addressing health and sustainability challenges through the integration of advanced technology and space-based data. Discover how AI and satellite imagery come together to uncover new insights.

The scarcity of reliable data in many regions presents significant challenges for health and sustainability monitoring. Concurrently, we see an explosion of available high-resolution satellite imagery. This research aims to harness the power of machine learning to extract valuable insights from such imagery, addressing global challenges like air quality monitoring, health indicators mapping, and more. For collaborations or insights, feel free to contact me.

Improving spatial agreement in machine learning-based landslide susceptibility mapping
  • Despite the high accuracy in landslide predictions from certain models, discrepancies exist leading to uncertainties. It's imperative to manage such natural phenomena with precise models.
